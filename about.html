A cruise line built to keep the peace.

Project Cruise was chartered by the Inter-Realm Commerce Council to give magical civilizations a neutral place to travel, trade, relax, and talk before tensions become wars.

Our mission

Tourism with a diplomatic purpose.

Project Cruise is not only a vacation. It is a floating meeting-ground where old enemies can share meals, attend envoy talks, and see each other as guests instead of threats.

Success means fewer unregulated wars, lower tensions, and fewer deaths between realms. Every route, cabin, and verification step supports that goal.

Rules of neutral water

The Accord

The Accord keeps the ship from becoming another realm's battlefield.

Neutral waters

At sea, beyond any realm's waters, the ship runs under its own charter: the Accord. No single realm rules the deck.

Realm waters

Within a realm's waters, that realm's laws apply. Guests receive port rules before arrival.

Verified boarding

The Veil Lens confirms each guest's true nature at booking and again at the gangway.

Business model

Peace still has to be profitable.

Project Cruise uses all-inclusive fares in crowns. One fare covers the cabin, meals to the guest's nature, port calls, and verification. No hidden fees.

Profit comes from premium species-tier cabins, envoy and diplomatic charters, and repeat tourism as realms learn to trust the ship.
